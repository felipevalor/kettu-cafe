### Context
- **Feature/Flow Name:** Kettu Café Landing Page
- **Target Audience State:** Local customers (Barrio Martin), coffee enthusiasts, returning visitors looking for the menu.
- **Core Objective:** Establish local brand presence, facilitate menu discovery, and drive delivery/contact.

### 1. The Executive Summary
- **The Verdict:** The landing page must bridge the gap between a high-end specialty coffee brand and a friendly neighborhood spot. The "Artesanal/Texturizado" approach is perfect for minimizing cognitive distance while maintaining a premium feel.
- **Top 2 Conversion Drivers:** 
  1. **Visual Relief:** High-quality food photography + warm tones to trigger immediate appetite and comfort.
  2. **Clarity of Intent:** One-click menu access and clear location/hours to reduce friction for on-the-go customers.

### 2. Behavioral & UX Breakdown
- **Visual Hierarchy & Flow:** Z-pattern header (Logo -> CTA) followed by F-pattern menu categories.
- **Hidden Mechanics:** Use of "Fox" branding as a guide/mascot to create a sense of belonging and brand personality.
- **Critical Friction Points:** Overwhelming menu selection. We will use *progressive disclosure* (categories) to avoid choice paralysis.

### 3. Actionable Improvements (Trackable Tasks)

**🟢 High Impact / Low Effort (Quick Wins)**
- [x] **UX-FIX-1.1 [Copy]**: Use "Ver la Carta de Hoy" (Check Today's Menu) instead of just "Menú" to create a sense of freshness and daily artisan work.
- [x] **UX-FIX-1.2 [Hierarchy]**: Keep the "Hacer Pedido" button sticky on mobile for immediate conversion.

**🟡 Medium to High Impact / Medium Effort (Structural Changes)**
- [x] **UX-NEW-1.1 [Trust Elements]**: Add the "Barrio Martin" mention prominently to anchor the brand to its physical community.
- [x] **UX-NEW-1.2 [Menu Flow]**: Implement a tabbed interface for categories to reduce vertical scroll fatigue.

**🔴 Strategic Overhauls (Long-term)**
- [ ] **UX-STRAT-1.1 [Architecture]**: Future integration with an online payment/ordering system once the artisan throughput allows.

### 4. Hypothesis for A/B Testing
- **Hypothesis:** "If we change the Hero CTA from 'Menú' to 'Ver la Carta (Artesanal)', then the Click-Through Rate will improve by 15% because it aligns with the brand's 'Relief' and 'Mastery' psychological drivers."
